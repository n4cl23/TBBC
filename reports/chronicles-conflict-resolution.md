# Conflict resolution review

## Decision

The four original blocking groups remain **excluded** from Batch 1. No source asset was copied, derived, uploaded or published.

| Group | Evidence | Resolution | Batch 1 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| brotherhood-of-hunters | Source folder contains at least five named people absent from the Canon, shared generic banner names, and numeric PNG variants. The canonical faction match does not establish member identities. | Keep every child asset as a candidate for separate identity/provenance review. | Excluded |
| durgan | Two labels (Durgan / Durgan Emberhand), multiple renders, and slicer/printer files occur together. The Canon confirms Durgan but does not prove the alternate label or select an official render. | Preserve both source sets; review identity and visual direction before any derivative. | Excluded |
| ironhold | The realm folder contains many creature candidates not present in the Canon and generic `Model #1.glb` names. A realm match does not create creature identities. | Keep assets source-only and split candidates only after Canon governance decisions. | Excluded |
| the-last-dragon-slayer | Two distinct PNG/STL pairs and a hollowing/slicer file exist. The Canon confirms the character but does not choose either visual set. | Preserve both sets; select artwork only after editorial approval. | Excluded |

## Non-blocking source note

The conflict labels are filename/provenance warnings, not claims that any source is invalid. They prevent accidental canonical overwrite.
